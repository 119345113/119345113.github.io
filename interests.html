Sport

I have won two Leinster Schools Junior Leagues as well as a Senior League with my secondary school Newbridge
College at a high level of rugby over the years which has really helped and developed my teamwork skills from a
very young age. I have also won county titles in Gaelic football with my local GAA club Athgarvan. I also
play golf at my local golf club The Royal Curragh Golf Club. I mostly play in summer and play off a handicap
of 14. I have won many tournaments over the years and have represented my county for Pitch & Putt twice.
I enjoy pushing myself physcially and mentally to become the best player I can be in all sports and for this
reason I also go to the gym regularly. Since joining UCC last September I have joined the Fresher football team
where I train regularly and compete in competitions with them.
